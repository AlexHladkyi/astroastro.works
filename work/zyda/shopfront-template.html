Shopfront template for order processing service provider

Brief:

Refresh the design of a shopfront template

Overview

Largely based around the material design system, the template is designed with simplicity in mind. The purpose is to allow small local business get their online shopfront up and running as fast as possible.

In the same vein, visually the template limits adjustability to a top image, logo and accent colour.

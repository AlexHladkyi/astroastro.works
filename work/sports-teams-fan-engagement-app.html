Sports teams fans engagement app for Fannex

Brief:

Improve the look and feel of mobile apps that the client provides to venues, events and sports teams. The new apps must use existing backend infrastructure. Also the new design must be flexible enough to allow for adjusting the styling to suite a particular team if they so desire.

Fannex provides interactive entertainment to attending spectators and fans of sporting events.

Overview

Since the new app had to work with existing structure of the information, it was decided to go with a feed-like main screen. This allowed us to keep the structure but also pull more information to the main screen that would otherwise be confined to secondary screens.

Header & gameday sections

For the new apps a dynamic header and gameday sections were introduced. Depending on whether there is a game announced, the user is at the venue or checked-in, these sections adjust their content to better suit the context.

Check-in, lists, articles, etc.

Customisation

Per client's request, the design was to be able to handle some limited customisation like colours, fonts, icons. This would allow sports teams to change the look of the app to represent team or brand colours. To showcase this feature a few examples were prepared.

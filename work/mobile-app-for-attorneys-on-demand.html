Mobile app for Attorneys on Demand

Brief:

Design a companion mobile app that would work alongside Attorneys on Demand web-based service.

Attorneys on Demand is a court appearance service that provides hearing coverage across the US.

Overview

The app presents relevant cases to attorneys, notifies them if the cases they have accepted were updated or cancelled and allows them to submit court hearing results.

Available, updated & accepted cases

New cases are placed on the Available Cases screen. Cases accepted by an attorney are moved to My Cases, also when an accepted case gets updated or cancelled it shows up on the Updates screen.

Case details

Case details show additional information about a given case. Main actions are placed at the bottom of the screen for easier reachability and one-handed use. Updated information appears highlighted until the attorney accepts the changes. Completed cases also show the information that was attached to the case by the attorney.

Completing a case

When completing a case, attorneys add notes and attach photos of the relevant documents. To go with the principle of invaluable user input, anything an attorney types or attaches is stored until the case is complete.
